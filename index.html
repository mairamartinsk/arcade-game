The Lost Jewels

    How to Play:

    Princess Lila needs your help!

    The Crown's Jewels got lost in the palace's gardens. Help Lila collect all the lost Jewels, but avoid getting bitten by the bugs!

    Once you collect all Jewels, get the Key to the palace's safe. You have 3 chances to help Lila.
